About

Prof. Ass. Dr. Jusuf Qarkaxhija was born on May 16, 1967, in Gjakova. He graduated in Electronics (University of Prishtina, 1992), a degree now equivalent to a Master of Science in Electronics. He also earned a Master of Science in Management and Informatics. In October 2015, he defended his doctoral dissertation at the European University of Tirana titled “Broadband Internet and E-Commerce: The Case of Kosovo.”

He has held roles across academia and industry including Dean of the Faculty of Computer Science at AAB College since 2017, Director of the AAB College branch in Gjakova (2014–2017), Coordinator of the Faculty of Economics at AAB College – Gjakova (2012–2014), and lecturer positions at AAB College, the University of Prizren, and vocational training institutions.
